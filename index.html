La Mirabelle

Function halls and Cafe

Function Halls in Oslo

La Mirabelle is a family-run business, that started as a cafe in 1999, and over time expanded. The doors of the new venture and the premises opened for the first time in 2007, where we as a business team and not least as a family have worked up to be able to arrange the perfect events for you. Our event premises are centrally located in Oslo, specifically, at Bjørvika.

Welcome and experience a completely new way of organizing events. We look forward to hearing from you!
