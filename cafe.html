LA MIRABELLA

Tradition since 1999

Here we want to serve you the best food, made from the best raw materials. We want to put you as a guest first and foremost do everything we can to ensure that you receive the best service we can provide. The customer must be the focus, we welcome compliments and praise and listen to your opinions on food and service and welcomes suggestions for improvements. We want to solve any problems in best way possible for our customers.




Our cafe have an extensive experience within cooking of all sizes, and as usual we always focus on quality and details. We adapt the menu as desired and ensure that the delicious food is delivered at. Our kitchen prepares the food and drinks you would need throughout the day. Everything from beautiful fruit platters to delicious buttered rolls and cake platters. We of course adapt for allergy sufferers or other special needs.




La Mirabella is located on Bjørvika and the restaurant has an intimate and pleasant atmosphere, with a fireplace that warms up on cold days. In the summer, we have a large outdoor terrace in historic surroundings. Our menu is based on modern cuisine, and we go to great lengths to use local and short-traveled goods. Welcome to us for delicious food and good service!
