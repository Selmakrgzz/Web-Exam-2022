Fake news all over

Fake news can be defined as news that is presented as factual news, as in true information given to the consumers, but is in fact false and misleading information. Fake news is often used in a way to damage the reputation of a person or entity, or to make money through advertisements. When producing fake news, rhetorical devices are often used to capture attention and create trust within the consumers. Fake news can therefore be very effective as a tool in various types of influence work.

With increased knowledge of media use and access to various types of information technology, it has become easier to produce and convey such representations now than in the past.

Credible Source

Digital developments have led to an increased threat of fake news. It is becoming increasingly difficult to distinguish between what is true from news and information online. As individuals in a society, we can refrain from sharing misinformation that we cannot trace back to a safe and credible source. Next, we should really think about what it is we are being informed about. Simple information online can sometimes seem very strong and influential. Therefore, it would be best to examine the sender more closely. All of this means that we have a critical understanding of the media, which enables us to use the media in a proven way, as well as combat the spread of fake news.

Society Together

There is always a certain purpose as to why there is fake news posted on the internet, some of these reasons could be that the website wants to defraud you, influence your political opinion, etc. Therefore, as an individual, you can follow some tips that make it easier to expose fake news. This method can be thinking and checking. First of all, you can think about whether the case seems too incredible, is it, for example, too catchy to be true? Next step, you should take a look at who is behind the source itself, here you should take a look at the website address itself. Also check a look at the pictures, fake news usually includes images taken from other cases or news.

Use image search to see where the image originated. Finally, you should double check with other sources to see if the source is credible. If the case is not found in several places, it can be suggested that it may be fake news.
